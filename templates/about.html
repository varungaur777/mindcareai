About MindCare AI

MindCare AI is a mental wellness prototype designed as an academic project. It combines mood assessment, journaling, simple AI-style conversation, and visual analytics to encourage self-reflection and emotional awareness.

The system is not a replacement for professional mental health care. Instead, it aims to demonstrate how technology can support early reflection, mood tracking, and gentle coping suggestions.

Technologies used: Python (Flask), SQLite, HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, Chart.js.
